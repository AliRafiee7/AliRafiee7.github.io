About Me

Computer Vision & Deep Learning Developer

Hi! My name is Ahmad Ali Rafiee and I am a researcher in the field of computer science. I received my master's degree in Electrical Engineering, specializing in image processing and computer vision, from Shiraz University in 2020 and have been working as a researcher at Shiraz University since then. My research interests include computer vision, image processing, deep learning, and machine learning.

I have done miscellaneous projects in the area of computer vision and image processing. Moreover, I have teaching experience in OpenCV, and I was the graduate teaching assistant for the image processing, pattern recognition, and advanced engineering mathematics courses.
